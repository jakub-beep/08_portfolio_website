Description

Project "Frames" aim was to design way of mounting suspension to body of the solar car "Klara". Althoug our body car made through lamination process and consists of carbon there was no way to prove strength of the body so the decision was made to create steel frames. In this way we got very safe steel structures.


Motivation


As a member of PUT SOLAR DYNAMICS and a master in enginnering to be I've been taking part in process of suspension design. One of my first assignments was to design way of mounting suspension to body of the car. Moreover it was a viable part of my master thesis.


Frames - specification

  • Mass - front frame: 28kg
  • Mass - rear frame: 21kg
  • Material: Steel S355
  • Dimensions - front frame[mm]: 858 x 355 x 405
  • Dimensions - rear frame[mm]: 798 x 187 x 425
  • Yield point of the material [MPa]: 355
  • Cross-sections of the pipes [mm]: 30x30x3
  • Checked for the forces from the ground
