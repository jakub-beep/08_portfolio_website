Description

Project "Roll Cage" was designed to provide safety for passengers of Solar Car "Klara". Its main purpose is to ensure the safety of the occupants during the use of the vehicle, especially during collisions. It is a steel, welded frame that covers the entire passenger compartment.


Motivation


As a member of PUT SOLAR DYNAMICS and a master in enginnering to be I've been looking for main project for my master thesis. During our preparations for a tournament we found out that according to its regulations we had to enforce our safety structures to participate in race. Because I've already had experience in FEA calculations and CAD modelling I've decided that I will do it.


Roll Cage - specification

  • Mass: 59kg
  • Material: Steel 25CrMo4
  • Dimensions[mm]: 1985 x 1960 x 1045
  • Yield point of the material [MPa]: 700
  • Cross-sections of the pipes [mm]: 40 x 2; 35 x 1.5; 30 x 1.5
  • Checked for the pressure 31 000 N/m during front, back, side and roll over collisions
  • Absorbing material:RRS FIA
