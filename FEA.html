Description

Project "Simulations" consists of the simulations I've performed during my time as a mechanical designer. There are here strength linear as well non-linear simulations of frames, roll-cage, underground reservois and car suspension.


Motivation

My first project connected with simulations was designing bike frame with necessary tests which happened to be my engineers thesis. As a suspension member in PUT SOLAR DYNAMICS I've been busy with suspension designing which included simulations connected with car behaviour on the road, which was necessary to design safe suspension. Moreover those researches and roll cage tests were part of my master thesis. As a mechanical designer during my job I've used to test underground reservois and frames to design the the lightest and the strongest reservois.


FEA - simulations

  • Front Frame
  • Rear Frame
  • Bike Frame
  • Car suspension
  • Multiple underground reservoirs with frames
