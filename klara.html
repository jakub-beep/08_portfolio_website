My contribution to the project


My journey with PUT SOLAR DYNAMICS started in 2019 when my friend told me that he is about to join some great science organisation, so I had to find out something more about that. I went to their workshop to just chat and well I stayed for years and even now after graduating I am still trying to help a little bit!


Firstly I was one of mechanical designers responsible for designing roll cage and parts of suspension. Later I've become leader of mechanical department and I was taking care of managing all mechanical members, recruiting new members, writing reports, making balance sheet and prognosis for future. And now as a graduate I am helping with some mechanical projects and creating website for our organization.
